Yukon Sourdough Rendezvous

Whitehorse, Yukon

The Yukon Sourdough Rendezvous is a 14 day annual celebration from February 8 – 24th. The Rendezvous was created as a celebration to the near ending of a long winter. The event has a Gold Rush theme which brings in many tourists. The Gold Rush in the Yukon was between 1896 – 1899. It was a migration of about 100,000 prospectors from all over the world who traveled on foot through the Dyea and Skagway route to try their luck finding gold after hearing about the amounts of people striking it rich in the Yukon.

Fun Rendezvous Activities

There are family friendly events and competitions such as:

  1. Ice scupltures competition
  2. Colour war competition
  3. 13,000km sled dog race
  4. Pet parade
  5. Sourdough Sam and Rendezvous Queen
  6. Chainsaw Chucking competition
  7. Dog packing competition

There are other family fun events such as:

  1. Food vendors
  2. Fireworks
  3. Jet plane show
  4. Can Can Dancers

There is adult themed parties such as:

  1. Berlesque shows
  2. Pub Crawl
